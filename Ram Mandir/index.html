RAM MANDIR



About:

Translating to Ram's Birthplace, the Ram Janmabhoomi is believed to have been the birthplace of the Hindu deity, Lord Ram. The temple complex is envisioned to be a grand architectural marvel, designed to accommodate millions of pilgrims and devotees from around the world. Today, Ram Janmabhoomi Ayodhya continues to be a site of immense religious significance and pilgrimage for Hindus worldwide. It stands as a testament to the enduring legacy of Lord Rama and the rich cultural heritage of India.

Fun Facts !

  1. The construction of the Ram Temple at Ram Janmabhoomi Ayodhya commenced in 2020 and was completed in January 22, 2024.
  2. On the first day of its opening, following the consecration, the temple received a rush of over half a million visitors,and after a month, the number of daily visitors was reported to be between 100,000 and 150,000.
  3. The Ram Mandir in Ayodhya is designed in traditional Nagar architectural style. It is one of the two main styles of Hindu temple architecture.
  4. The main temple of Ayodhya is surrounded by four temples dedicated to Sun God, Goddess Bhagwati, Lord Ganesha, and Lord Shiva.
